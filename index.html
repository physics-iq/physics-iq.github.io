About Physics-IQ

Physics-IQ is a video understanding benchmark designed to evaluate the ability of AI systems to reason about physical interactions and temporal events. This project introduces a novel dataset, evaluation metrics, and tools to facilitate groundbreaking research in video understanding.

Benchmarks

Physics-IQ provides a comprehensive set of evaluation metrics and baseline models to benchmark AI performance in video understanding tasks. Our benchmark includes action recognition, temporal segmentation, and multi-view consistency evaluation.
